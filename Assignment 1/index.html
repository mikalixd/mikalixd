The Development

Built upon the hillside of Fo Tan in Sha Tin and overlooking the scenery of Sha Tin Town Centre, the development was occupied by Hong Kong Baptist University (HKBU) in the autumn of 1994. At present, Towers 1, 2 and 3 are used as the staff quarters of the University with a total of 68 units in the three towers.
